🧠 Mindful Eating for Blood Sugar

Mindful eating means paying full attention to the experience of eating: the taste, texture, and smell of food, your hunger and fullness cues, and the pace at which you eat. It is not a diet — it is a way of eating that helps you make better choices and enjoy food more.
