⚖️ Weight and Insulin Resistance

Excess weight, especially around the abdomen, increases insulin resistance — meaning your body needs more insulin to control blood sugar. Even modest weight loss (5-7% of body weight, about 10-14 lbs for a 200 lb person) can significantly improve insulin sensitivity, lower A1C, and reduce medication needs.
