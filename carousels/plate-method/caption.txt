🍽️ The Diabetes Plate Method

The diabetes plate method is one of the simplest ways to build balanced meals. Use a 9-inch plate and divide it into three sections: half non-starchy vegetables, one quarter lean protein, and one quarter carbohydrates.

  2. Non-Starchy Vegetables (Half the Plate)
  3. Protein (One Quarter)
  4. Carbohydrates (One Quarter)
